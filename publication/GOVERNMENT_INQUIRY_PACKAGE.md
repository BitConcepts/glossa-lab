## 3. Methodology Statement

The Glossa-Lab CGSA methodology is a purely data-driven approach to Indus script analysis. It:

1. Ingests the published Indus corpus from established academic sources (CISI, Yajnadevam)
2. Applies deterministic statistical clustering to identify sign positional classes
3. Validates structural classes across all 52 known Indus Valley sites
4. Tests structural hypotheses against published non-linguistic baselines (Nair 2026)
5. Uses substitution analysis to test candidate language compatibility

No phonetic assignments have been made from external assumptions. All findings are derived from the inscriptions themselves.
